The Artist

Meet Sanja

Transforming threads into cherished companions, one handmade creation at a time.

From her cozy studio in Novi Sad, Serbia, Sanja Krsti\u0107 discovered a passion for crochet that quickly turned into magical TikTok live sessions. What started as quiet evenings with yarn has blossomed into a community of thousands who watch in awe as thread transforms into adorable plush friends before their eyes.

Every piece tells a story — of patience, creativity, and the joy of handmade craftsmanship. When you hold a Sanja creation, you're holding hours of live-streamed artistry, made exclusively for you.

The Journey

From Thread to Treasure

01

Premium Yarn Selection

Carefully selecting the softest, highest-quality yarns. Every fiber is chosen for texture, durability, and comfort.

Hand Stitching

Each piece is meticulously crocheted stitch by stitch during live TikTok sessions, transforming flat yarn into 3D art.

03

Details & Finishing
